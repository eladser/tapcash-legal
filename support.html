Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the Shop tab and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed into the same Google Play or Apple ID used for the original purchase.

I lost my game progress. Can you recover it?

If you were signed into Google Play Games (Android) or Game Center (iOS), your progress may be recoverable. Go to Settings > Account & Cloud Save and sign in. If you played as a guest, unfortunately we cannot recover local data.

How do I cancel my VIP subscription?

Android: Open Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions > TapCash Empire > Cancel
iOS: Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > TapCash Empire > Cancel

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Google Play or Apple, not by us directly. Visit the respective app store's refund page or contact their support.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings in the game, scroll down to "Data Management", and tap "Delete All Game Data". This action cannot be undone.

The game is running slowly. What can I do?

Try closing other apps, restarting your device, or reinstalling the game (make sure cloud save is enabled first!). The game works best on devices from 2018 or newer.

Is this game free to play?

Yes! TapCash Empire is free to download and play. There are optional in-app purchases for gems, special items, and VIP subscriptions, but you can enjoy the full game without spending money.
