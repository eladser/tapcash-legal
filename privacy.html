Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2026

Summary: We collect minimal data necessary to provide the game experience. We do not sell your personal information. This game is for users aged 13 and older.

1. Introduction

eladS Studio ("we", "our", or "us") operates TapCash Empire (the "Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Game.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you sign in with Google Play Games or Apple Game Center, we receive your display name and user ID for cloud save functionality.
  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases, payment processing is handled by Google Play or Apple. We receive confirmation of purchases but not payment details.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Game Progress: Your game state, achievements, and settings are saved locally and optionally synced to cloud services.
  • Analytics Data: We collect anonymous usage data (session duration, feature usage, purchase events) to improve the game experience.
  • Device Information: Basic device type and OS version for compatibility purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Game
  • Sync your game progress across devices
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the Game
  • Provide customer support

4. Third-Party Services

Our Game uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics: For anonymous usage analytics. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: For in-app purchase management. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Games Services: For Android cloud saves and achievements. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Game Center: For iOS cloud saves and achievements. Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

We retain your game data as long as you use the Game. You can delete your data at any time through the in-game settings menu under "Delete All Game Data".

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

TapCash Empire is intended for users aged 13 and older. The game contains simulated gambling elements (wheel spinner) and is not suitable for younger children.

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • Users must confirm they are 13 or older before playing
  • Users who indicate they are under 13 are blocked from accessing the game
  • We do not collect any personal information from children under 13

If you are a parent and believe your child has bypassed the age verification, please contact us and we will delete any associated data.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics collection
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below or use the in-app settings.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption of saved game data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the Game or by updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
